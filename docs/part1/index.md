## Part 1 Learning Outcomes

By the end of Part 1 you will be able to:

- [x] Define Knowledge Engineering and explain its purpose
- [x] Describe the history and evolution of KE from Expert Systems to modern hybrid AI
- [x] Classify different types of knowledge and explain why the distinction matters
- [x] Map out the full KE lifecycle and identify the role of each phase
- [x] Explain the architecture of an Expert System and trace how it reasons
- [x] Identify the Knowledge Acquisition Bottleneck and describe modern solutions

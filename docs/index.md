## What is This Course?

**Knowledge Engineering (KE)** is the discipline of capturing, structuring, and applying human expertise in computer systems — enabling machines to reason, make decisions, and solve complex problems with accuracy and explainability.

This course takes you from the foundations of KE all the way through to building production-grade, cloud-native KE systems that combine structured knowledge with modern Generative AI.

---

## Who Is This Course For?

| Audience | What You Will Gain |
|---|---|
| **Software Engineers** | Build intelligent, rule-driven backend systems |
| **Solution Architects** | Design KE-powered cloud architectures |
| **Business Analysts** | Understand how to capture and structure domain knowledge |
| **AI/ML Engineers** | Add explainability and structure to AI pipelines |
| **IT Professionals** | Apply KE to automate complex domain decisions |

**Prerequisites:** Basic programming knowledge, familiarity with databases, interest in AI/intelligent systems.
